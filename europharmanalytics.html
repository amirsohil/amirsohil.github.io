EuroPharm Analytics

Dashboard

Created as an entry for the January–February 2026 DataDNA – Pharmacy Sales & Profitability Analytics Challenge, EuroPharm Analytics is a system that translates complex pharmacy sales data into clear, defensible decisions around margin quality, product strategy, and operational focus. It combines business logic, statistical benchmarking, and visual design to evaluate not just how the business is performing, but why, and what can be done to improve it.
