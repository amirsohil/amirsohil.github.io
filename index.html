About Myself

A second year undergraduate following the BSc (Hons) Data Science Programme offered by Coventry University, United Kingdom, I have obtained an Advanced Diploma in Data Science (with a Distinction) with a gold medal for best results from the National Institute of Business Management, Sri Lanka. I have also obtained a Certificate in Business Accounting from the Chartered Institute of Management Accountants (CIMA).

Growing up, data science was the ultimate dream field of work for me. I was a person who grew up with a very strong connection to understanding how and why things work the way they do, which prompted me to choose data science as a career path. I am currently on my way to realizing my childhood dream.

I have around five years of experience in programming, having worked extensively with languages including Python, R, SQL, etc., during said period. I have hands-on experience in Data Mining, Cleaning, Statistical Analyses, Visualizations, Machine Learning and Big Data Analytics. In addition to these, I have some experience in Fullstack Development as well.
