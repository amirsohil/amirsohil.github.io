All Goals in the Men's World Cup

Dashboard

This visual is a comprehensive exploration of all 2,720 goals scored in the Men's World Cup across 22 tournaments, presented through a design built around one of sport's most recognisable symbols—the FIFA World Cup Trophy. The aim was to merge historical depth with a bold visual identity, transforming nine decades of football data into an intuitive, engaging, and emotionally resonant experience.
