About

A brief overview about myself.

I am Amir. I code and visualise data. I am skilled in machine learning and analytics, being particularly known for my visualisations and storytelling. My projects have won prizes in global open data competitions and have been featured in national exhibitions in my home country, Sri Lanka.

Education-wise, I graduated as the batch topper from Coventry University, holding a First Class in BSc (Hons) Data Science. With over two and a half years of professional experience in data science and analytics, I have acquired hands-on expertise in a wide array of essential areas, including data mining, data cleaning, statistical analysis, visualisation, machine learning, deep learning, and big data analytics.

I am a passionate football enthusiast, with unwavering support for Liverpool FC since the age of eleven. Beyond cheering from the sidelines, I also actively participate in the beautiful game, having represented both my school and university across numerous tournaments as a left winger/center midfielder.
